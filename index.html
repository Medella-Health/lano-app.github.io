THE FUTURE OF TEAR DIAGNOSTICS



When they said you can look into your body via your eyes, they didn’t see this coming. The tear diagnostics industry, has grown to $5 billion. Today, with a drop of tear,
one can analyze the variety of bio-markers that tell us about our
ocular and systemic diseases.



Introducing
LANO

Lano is the ultimate tear diagnostic platform that analyzes the glucose and osmolarity concentrations with a single drop of tear.









PAINLESS


Lano works by taking a drop of
tear from the patient (2-4 uL) with
a simple & easy-to-use strip.

MULTIPLE BIO-MARKERS


Our hub detects multiple bio-markers without running multiple tests, so clinicians can spend more time
focusing on the patients


DATA ANALYTICS


Our HIPPA compliant software
creates reports for clinicians
to track and review patient
health, and can be viewed
from any secure device.

POINT-OF-CARE


Provides the results to the clinician within minutes of running the test, without the need of a second check-up.

SEAMLESS INTEGRATION


Because Lano is a desktop point-of-care device, it integrates seamlessly into your existing practise with a simple on-boarding process. Also, did we mention – we provide technical support during all business hours (ET).
